# BreakingBricksNews

A community-contributed [YakYak](https://yakyak.ai/) channel — a satirical,
brick-built "breaking news" show that turns everyday absurdities into episodic news
segments.

## What's here

- The exported campaign (`workflow/export-campaign` JSON) so you can
  [import](https://api.yakyak.ai/api/docs) it into your own account
  (`workflow/import-campaign`) or fork it (`workflow/fork-campaign`).
- Notes on the style, cast, and settings that give the channel its look and voice.

## Try it

1. Import or fork the campaign into your YakYak account.
2. Adjust the premise, cast, or style to taste.
3. Generate and render the next episode, then post it to your socials.

See [`../README.md`](../README.md) for the community-contributions overview and
[`../../docs/`](../../docs/) for how the campaign → movie → scene model works.
